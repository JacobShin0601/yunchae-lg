## Key Findings Summary

1. **Battery Pack Costs**:
   - 2025: $115/kWh (projected)
   - 2026: $100/kWh (projected price parity threshold)
   - 2030: $67/kWh (projected)

2. **Price Parity Timeline**:
   - **Base Case**: 2033 (8% annual battery cost reduction, $7,500 incentives)
   - **Optimistic**: 2027 (12% annual battery cost reduction, $10,000 incentives)
   - **Breakthrough**: 2026 (15% annual battery cost reduction, $12,500 incentives)

3. **Vehicle Segment Impact**:
   - Small EVs (50 kWh): 2030
   - Medium EVs (75 kWh): 2033
   - Large EVs (100 kWh): After 2035

4. **Government Incentives Impact**:
   - No Incentives: After 2035
   - $7,500 (Current US Federal): 2033
   - $10,000 (Enhanced): 2028
   - $12,500 (Maximum): 2026

5. **Regional Variations**:
   - Earliest: California (2024)
   - Latest: Southern US (2028)
