## Analysis Stage: North America EV Market Price Parity Analysis
## Execution Time: 2025-07-30 15:48:41
--------------------------------------------------
Result Description: 
This analysis examined the price parity between electric vehicles (EVs) and internal combustion engine (ICE) vehicles in the North American market, with a focus on battery cost trends.

Key findings:
1. Battery pack costs have declined from $1,200/kWh in 2010 to $115/kWh in 2025
2. Price parity is expected to be achieved across all vehicle segments by 2024-2025
3. With government incentives, price parity has already been achieved since 2020
4. Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) parity is achieved earlier than purchase price parity
5. Price elasticity of demand for EVs averages around -2.0
6. Battery costs are projected to fall below $100/kWh by 2026-2027

The analysis included historical data analysis, price parity modeling, TCO calculations, and market adoption scenarios based on price elasticity.
